Mujahadah Al-Nafs

The Battle Within

Every believer faces two voices. One calls to temptation, and the other calls toward Allah. Mujāhadah al-nafs is the fight inside our hearts that no one sees except Allah.

الْمُجَاهِدُ مَنْ جَاهَدَ نَفْسَهُ فِي طَاعَةِ اللَّهِ
"The mujahid is the one who strives against his own self in obedience to Allah."
(Mishkat al-Masabih 33, 34)

3 Takeaways from the Khutbah

💖 1. The real jihad is inside you
Resisting temptation, lowering your gaze, and avoiding sin in private are victories that no one else may see, but Allah rewards greatly.
💫 2. The soul has levels
An-Nafs Al-Ammārah – the soul that inclines to evil.
An-Nafs Al-Lawwāmah – the soul that feels guilt and repents.
An-Nafs Al-Muṭmaʾinnah – the soul that is calm and content with Allah.
🌿 3. Strive in small daily battles
Pray even when tired, control anger, forgive others, and speak kindly. Every act of control is a step closer to Allah.

Qur'an Reminder

يَا أَيَّتُهَا النَّفْسُ الْمُطْمَئِنَّةُ ارْجِعِي إِلَىٰ رَبِّكِ رَاضِيَةً مَرْضِيَّةً
"O you tranquil soul, return to your Lord, well pleased and pleasing to Him."
(Surah Al-Fajr 89:27–28)

Reflection

Think about your own nafs. What do you struggle with most? Anger, pride, laziness, or desire? Choose one thing this week to control for the sake of Allah.
Du'a
اللَّهُمَّ آتِ نَفْسِي تَقْوَاهَا، وَزَكِّهَا أَنْتَ خَيْرُ مَنْ زَكَّاهَا، أَنْتَ وَلِيُّهَا وَمَوْلَاهَا
O Allah, give my soul its righteousness and purify it. You are the best to purify it. You are its Guardian and Protector.
